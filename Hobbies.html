Origami is a form of japanese paper art which utilizes a uncut square peice of paper with only fold. Thre are diffrent kinds of origami such as tessilation origami which is a flat piece of paper that folded into patterns. You can use many diffrent sheets of paper that is thin and reliable. They are usually treated with methylcellulose to promote a crispy feeling in the paper. This also prevents the fraying on the fold likes which makes it fuzzy and unasthetic.
